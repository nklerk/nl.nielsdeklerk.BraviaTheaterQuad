Before you begin

This app requires External Control to be enabled on your Bravia Theater Quad. Please follow these steps first:

  1. 1 Open the Bravia Connect app on your phone
  2. 2 Select your Bravia Theater Quad
  3. 3 Go to Settings → Network Settings
  4. 4 Enable External Control
Without External Control enabled, the app cannot read or change any settings on your device.
